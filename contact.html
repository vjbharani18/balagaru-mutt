About Madhwa Siddhanta

For those interested in learning about Dvaita Vedanta, the Mutt offers regular classes and discourses. The philosophy emphasizes the eternal distinction between the Supreme Lord Vishnu and individual souls, and teaches that liberation is achieved through devotion (Bhakti) and knowledge (Jnana) of the Lord's true nature.

How to Reach

By Road:

The mutt is accessible by road from Tirthahalli (4 KM), Shivamogga (46 KM), or Bangalore (307 KM).

Nearby Areas:

The mutt is located in the border region of Shimoga and Chikmagalur districts. Nearby villages include Melige, Melinakuruvalli, Bejjavalli, and Neraturu (all within 6 KM).
