About the Samsthanam

Sri Arya Akshobya teertha moola Samsthanam Balagaru (Balagaru Akshobhya Tirtha Mutt) sits quietly on the banks of the sacred river Tunga in Baalagaru village, near Tirthahalli. We maintain the Guru‚ÄìShishya parampara, offer Vedic study and serve the surrounding communities through seva and education.

This mutt is the Moola Mutt (root mutt) of Kangu Mutt. Following the Madhwa Siddhanta (Dvaita Vedanta) tradition established by Sri Madhvacharya, our Samsthanam upholds the philosophy of Bheda (difference) between the Supreme Lord Vishnu, individual souls (Jivas), and matter (Jada).

Guru Parampara

Our Parampara traces the succession of Acharyas who preserved the teachings of Madhwa Siddhanta. The lineage follows the tradition from Sri Madhvacharya (1238-1317 CE), who established Dvaita Vedanta.

Sacred Spaces & Activities

Explore the spiritual heritage and activities of the Samsthanam

Temple Sanctum

The sacred sanctum houses the main deities including Sri Madhwacharyara kararchita Sri Rama Devaru (Vaikunta Rama, Seetha & Lakshmana), Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Devaru, and the rare Vishwambhara Saligrama.

Mutt Premises

The mutt premises include the main hall, meditation areas, and the sacred Vrundavanas of the previous Acharyas. The architecture reflects traditional Dravidian style with modern amenities.

Daily Rituals

Experience the daily puja ceremonies, bhajans, and Vedic recitations. The mutt conducts regular yagnas and special ceremonies during festivals, following traditional Madhwa Siddhanta practices.
