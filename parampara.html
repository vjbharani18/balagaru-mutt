Guru Parampara

The Mutt honours the following Acharyas in succession. The list below is presented with reverence. The current Peetadhipati is highlighted.

  1. Srimat Venkatesha Bharati Swami
    Founder — established the line
  2. Srimat Ramachandra Tirtha
    Preserved the practice of Vedic teaching
  3. Srimat Balagaru Peetadhipati
    Current Peetadhipati — guiding the Mutt today
  4. Srimat Vishveshwara Saraswati
    Senior Acharya — teacher and scholar

Note: Names and titles are shown in a simple, respectful style. Update this page as the lineage grows.
