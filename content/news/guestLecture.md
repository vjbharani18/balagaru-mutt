On 10 May 2025, Srimat Vishveshwara Saraswati gave a lecture on key principles of Vedanta and their relevance to daily life. The talk emphasised sraddha (faith), shraddha (devotion), and study.

Highlights:
1. Importance of śāstra study.
2. Daily sadhana and seva.
3. Q&A with attendees.

Photos and notes are available in the Mutt's record room.
