A glimpse into the spiritual life and activities of the Samsthanam

Temple Sanctum

The sacred sanctum houses the main deities including Sri Madhwacharyara kararchita Sri Rama Devaru, Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Devaru, and the rare Vishwambhara Saligrama.

Mutt Premises

The mutt premises include the main hall, meditation areas, and the sacred Vrundavanas of the previous Acharyas. The architecture reflects traditional Dravidian style.

Narasimha Deity

The sacred Narasimha deity worshipped in the mutt, established by Sri Akshobya Teertharu. This deity is central to the spiritual practices of the Samsthanam.
