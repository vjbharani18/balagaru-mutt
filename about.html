About Balagaru Mutt

Balagaru Mutt is a traditional monastic institution (mutt) located in Teerthahalli. It is dedicated to preserving the Vedic teachings, the Guru–Shishya parampara and serving the local community.

History

The Mutt was established to serve as a centre for study and practice of dharma, with emphasis on scriptural learning, performance of ritual duties and community seva. Over the years the Mutt has supported students of Vedic learning and engaged in social welfare in nearby villages.

Philosophy & Purpose

Our purpose is to guide sincere seekers through traditional instruction, provide spiritual support to the community, and preserve the lineage of Acharyas who have safeguarded these teachings.
